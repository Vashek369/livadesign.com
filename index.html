Built to deliver.

LIVA was founded by Libor & Vašek — two designers with over 7 years of full-time, professional experience. They've been working together since high school, and that long-term collaboration is what drives the studio's consistency and trust.

The studio operates across the full range — from sharp, focused brand work to large-scale corporate projects. Young, technically aware, and up-to-date with where design and technology are heading. No client type is out of scope.

Behind every project is a well-structured, end-to-end process — combining design, development, and production into one seamless workflow. LIVA delivers complete packages, ensuring consistency, precision, and high-quality execution across every stage, regardless of project size.
